My Favorite Qoute


"Everything is not meant for everyone"

found in It's Okay Not to Be Okay by Sheila Walsh


The quote means that not everything in life is meant for everyone to have, achieve, or handle, and that's perfectly okay. Each person has unique strengths, paths, and purposes, so we shouldn’t feel pressured to meet every expectation or fit into roles that aren’t meant for us. It’s a reminder to embrace our individuality and focus on what truly matters to us.
